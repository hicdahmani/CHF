Our Central Question

"What kinds of knowledge, educational systems, cultures, and societies do we want to cultivate as intelligent technologies become integral to human life?"

This conference moves beyond assessing impacts or predicting outcomes toward intentional design and ethical imagination. We invite participants to articulate aspirations, examine trade-offs, and consider how intelligent systems might be shaped to support human flourishing, epistemic pluralism, educational equity, cultural vitality, and democratic life.

Interdisciplinary Dialogue

Bringing together scholars, educators, technologists, and practitioners to engage in critical and constructive conversations about our shared futures.

Beyond Prediction

Moving past assessment of impacts toward imagination, deliberation, and intentional design of the futures we are co-creating.

Epistemic Justice

Centering questions of whose knowledge matters, what voices are heard, and how to ensure epistemic pluralism in intelligent systems.

Human Flourishing

Examining how technology can support rather than undermine human flourishing, democratic life, and cultural vitality.
